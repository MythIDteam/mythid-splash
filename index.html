What Is MythID?
MythID is not a profile. It is a living archive of who you are, through what you wear, where you've been, and how you remember.

You don’t create content here. You create Lore.

Who Is It For?
- People who build identity through clothing
- People who thrift, stitch, salvage, remix
- People tired of being sold back to themselves

What You Can Do Here:
• Log Items
• Build Outfits
• Submit Lore
• Record Lore Places
• Assign Moods, Classes, Tags

What Is Verified Lore?
Verified outfits require proof, peer validation, and presence. Your Myth lives through you.

First Prompt:
What piece of clothing have you owned the longest?
Where has it gone with you?
Why have you kept it?
